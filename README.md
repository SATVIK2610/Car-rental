# Car-rental
Welcome to our Car Rental Service website! Here, you can easily book a car from a wide range of vehicles to suit all your travel needs. Our platform is designed to offer flexible rental times at feasible rates, ensuring a smooth and convenient experience for all users.

# Features
Wide Range of Vehicles: Choose from economy cars, luxury sedans, SUVs, and more to match your specific requirements.
Flexible Booking Options: Rent a car for as long as you need, whether it's a few hours, a day, a week, or even longer.
Feasible Rates: Enjoy competitive pricing with no hidden fees, making your rental experience both affordable and transparent.
User-Friendly Interface: Easily navigate through our website to find and book the perfect car for your journey.
